## What Ark99 is

Ark99 is a low-friction receiving field for seeds that are not yet formal projects.

It can hold early signals, naming candidates, lightweight concepts, and public-safe project entry notes.

Ark99 is not a final seal.
Ark99 is not automatic projectization.
Ark99 is a place where small seeds can be noticed without forcing them into a heavy structure too early.

## Second-Thread Gate

A key Ark99 signal is the Second-Thread Gate.

If the same seed, theme, or named pattern reaches a second thread, it becomes an Ark00 pickup candidate.

This does not mean immediate promotion.
It means the seed has shown enough continuity to be reviewed through a selective incubation gate.

## Signal / Skill direction

Ark99 watches for signals such as:

- repeated themes
- unexpected success
- reusable naming
- useful workflow patterns
- human-AI semi-automation loops

A signal may later become a skill if it becomes reusable and embodied in the workflow.
